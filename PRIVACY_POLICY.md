# Privacy Policy for Gym Manager Pro

**Last Updated: April 17, 2026**

This privacy policy applies to the Gym Manager Pro app (herein referred to as "the Application") for mobile devices that was created by Engr. Hamza Asad (herein referred to as "the Service Provider") as a Commercial service.

## Information Collection and Use

The Application collects and processes information to provide its core features. All member data, photos, and financial records are stored **locally** on your device.

### Permissions Requested
The Application may request the following permissions to function:
*   **Camera:** To take profile photos of members.
*   **Storage (Read/Write):** To save member photos and create/restore ZIP backups.
*   **Biometrics/Fingerprint:** To provide the App Lock security feature.
*   **Contacts/SMS:** To send fee reminders via WhatsApp or SMS.
*   **Location/Wi-Fi:** Used for the "Hotspot Sync" feature to connect two devices locally.

### Third-Party Services
The Application uses Google Play Services (via Google Sign-In and Google Drive API) for the following:
*   **Authentication:** To link your account for backup purposes.
*   **Backups:** To optionally save your encrypted ZIP backups to your own Google Drive.

The Service Provider does **not** have access to your data. All data remains between your device and your personal Google Drive account.

## Data Security
The Application includes an **App Lock** feature to prevent unauthorized access to the data stored on your device. We recommend enabling Biometric or PIN security within the app settings.

## Offline Functionality
The Application is designed to work fully offline. Data synchronization between devices happens over a local network (Hotspot) and does not transmit data to any external servers.
